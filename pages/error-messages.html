Error Messages

 

Error Type

How to fix it

Invalid username/password

Ensure that you have entered the correct username and password. If you don’t remember your password, click the Forgot Password button

Please enter a valid

Email Address

-Ensure your email address is typed out correctly and try again

Entered passwords do not match

Ensure that the password in the first text box is the same as the second one. These passwords are case sensitive

This email hasn’t been confirmed yet

Go to your email and look for the account verification email and click on the link

File is too big when uploading images or proof of payment

The system cannot take files of more than 4MB. Choose a file less than 4MB

Please select an Image

You cannot add a print job without adding an Image

Quantity cannot be less than zero

When selecting a quantity for your quotation request, make sure the quantity is more than one otherwise it is invalid.

Value cannot be more than 100

When writing a percentage for the business rules, make sure your values are less than 100
